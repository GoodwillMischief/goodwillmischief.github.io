We are a French CTF team passionate about cybersecurity. Since 2023, we have been regularly participating in team competitions, always with the aim of improving our skills and pushing our limits in this discipline.

The idea behind the Goodwill Mischief project is simple: to grow together while contributing to the community. The team’s name reflects our philosophy: a spirit of experimentation and curiosity (mischief), always guided by good intentions (goodwill).

If you also want to improve and share your passion for cybersecurity, feel free to join us! We have hidden <span class="hint">a small, easy, and quick challenge in the “Join Us” section</span> to test your skills right away. If you succeed, you’ll gain access to our Discord server. Be aware, the number of invitations is limited.
